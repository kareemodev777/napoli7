## Content Gaps Identified

1. **Location page**: lacks address, hours, phone, branch details (all live on Contact instead).
2. **Privacy policy**: contains terms content; needs real privacy policy.
3. **Terms & Conditions** + **Refund**: current pages are near-duplicates. Need clear separation.
4. **Product pages**: no allergens, no nutrition, no size options, no reviews.
5. **No real "Deals" inventory**: only one welcome offer; needs combo/bundle structure.
6. **No FAQ page**: FAQs are buried inside Contact.
7. **Track Order**: routes to `/my-account/orders/` — not a public guest tracker.
8. **No multi-language**: Dieci has DE/EN/FR/IT toggle; Napoli7 is EN-only. Add **AR** for Ajman audience.
