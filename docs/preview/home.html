Lievito madre. The slow art of dough.

Our dough is built on Caputo flour and a slow-fermented sourdough starter. Hand-stretched using schiaffo napoletano and finished in a 450°C oven. The result is a crust that's airy, charred, and unmistakably Neapolitan.

Our Story

Seven Pizzas. Done Right.

Napoli 7 is dedicated to authentic Neapolitan pizza in Ajman, inspired by the traditions of Naples — the birthplace of pizza. The name reflects our philosophy: a deliberately limited selection of seven pizzas to ensure perfect execution and consistently fresh ingredients.

"Tradition is not the worship of ashes — it is the keeping of fire."
— Founder, Napoli 7
